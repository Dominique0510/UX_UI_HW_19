Outboulder Desktop & Mobile Redesign

Outboulder is a Non profit Organization who helps the LGBTQ+ Community feel included, shares volunteer opportunities, showcases upcoming events, and so much more! During our research, we discovered that many of our Users had a difficult time navigating through the website and felt very overwhelmed with the amount of information provided. Please click on “View Case Study” to view our redesign!

Federal Trade Commission Homepage Redesign

The Federal Trade Commission is a valuable website to utilize if users ever experience fraud or identity theft. During user testing, we discovered that the majority of them felt very overwhelmed with all the information on the website, wanted the navigation bar to be condensed, and overall wanted to have a more calming feel when navigated through the website!

RAAD MOBILE REDESIGN

My group and I wanted to create a one-stop for any “Do It Yourself” craft lovers! When conducting our research, we discovered that our users find it frustrating that there aren’t any DIY apps that focus on their individual learning styles and how they wish they could purchase the materials needed for their projects straight from the app.
